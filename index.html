About

Hello! I’m Thanoshan Vijayanandan, a passionate Software Engineer from Sri Lanka, currently working at 99x. With a Bachelor’s degree in Computing and Information Systems, I’ve navigated the tech world fueled by my passion for Software Engineering and Artificial Intelligence. I’m always excited to explore innovative technologies and aim to make a meaningful impact in the industry.

As a PhD aspirant, my curiosity drives me to dive deep into the complexities of Software Engineering and AI, aiming to contribute innovative solutions. I believe in lifelong learning to keep up with new technologies. I enjoy collaborating with diverse teams to exchange insights and grow together.

I enjoy sharing my experiences and insights in technology through blogging, which allows me to reflect on my journey and engage with a wider audience. As an ASF Committer, I’m committed to promoting collaboration and innovation in the open-source community, supporting projects that inspire positive change.
